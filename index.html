About me

Hi, I'm Craig! I'm a second-year Operations Research PhD student at the University of Toronto, supervised by Professors Timothy Chan and Ningyuan Chen.

During my Masters, I explored a variety of sport decision problems using Markov processes and mixed-integer programming. Lately, I've been interested in developing economic and game theoretic models to solve real-world problems that can impact policy decisions.

In the Summer of 2021, I was a Data Scientist on Amazon's SCOT team.
