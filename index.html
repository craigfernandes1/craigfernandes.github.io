About me

Hi, I'm Craig Fernandes. I'm a first-year PhD student in the Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering at the University of Toronto under the supervision of Professor Timothy Chan. Previously, I completed my Master and Bachelor of Applied Science in Industrial Engineering at UofT. I also spent the Summer of 2021 as a Data Scientist for Amazon's Supply Chain Optimization Team .

I'm interested in applying economic utility techniques, game theory and stochastic process models to solve real-world problems that can impact policy decisions. I am also an avid sports fan and enjoy exploring various sports analytics problems.
